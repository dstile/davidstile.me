International objectives

-increase awareness in areas of greatest needs

-This will encourage a group mentality in the community
-Will promote entrepreneurship and hopefully lead to business solution providing increased revenue in the community

  • Year round occupation

-More long term programs will increase efficacy of programs and will make the sustainability more of a success

  • Compilation of a written documentation and surveys into a published report
