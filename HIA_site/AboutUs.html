RANCHO AL MEDIO

“We are a University of Michigan student non-profit organization dedicated to the development of sustainable programs for the communities of Rancho al Medio, Dominican Republic and latino groups in the Washtenaw, U.S. region "

We work with a community in the Dominican Republic filled with people showing promise and talent.  The community is called Rancho al Medio and the people need some guidance towards the proper resources and assistance in working through the country’s bureaucratic processes to improve the healthcare in the region.
