Health In Action will be presenting our work at this years GHEC Conference!

Health In Action Abstract:

Health in Action is an organization that creates health care education presentations to present in a rural community of the Dominican Republic during spring break. These programs cover a variety of educational topics, including basic health care, literacy in math and English, and engineering solutions to local problems. These programs focus on making sustainable changes within the community to improve their standard of living. True improvements to the community will only ever be made if the programs are sustainable. We attempt to make these programs sustainable by training leaders within the community that will hopefully continue to improve the well being of the others community members when we leave. To make our programs more sustainable in upcoming years we plan to have students in the Dominican Republic year round. In addition to the above mentioned projects, this year’s trip will also include a module on possible economic opportunities for community members presented by business school students and a team working on a community wide survey focusing on removing the barriers to community members receiving proper healthcare. In past years the focus of Health in Action has been to implement these programs to the same community in the Dominican Republic. This year for the first time we will be branching out and also be working on similar programs for impoverished local communities.
